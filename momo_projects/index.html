Background

I learned to crochet and cross stitch in my youth, however over the past 5 years I have ventured into more extensive projects. The earliest of the projects you will see here is the Pok√©mon cross-stitch. This project began while I was in college and recently after getting married, intended to eventually go in the room of our first child (goal accomplished!). The crochet projects started after that cross-stitch was done and I needed another creative outlet. My friend helped me learn how to follow patterns and since then I have been making project after project. Now, my crochet and ongoing cross-sttich projects are a good way to relax at the end of a long day of working in a research lab and raising a toddler.

Introduction

This website will show off the crochet and cross-stitch projects that I have created. Here you can get an idea of the kinds of projects that can be created and see my level of skill. Feel free to contact me with any questions that may arise as you browse my collection. You can find me on Facebook by clicking the Facebook logo at the bottom of the page.

Almost all of these projects were made using patterns found online. The links at the bottom of the pages displaying my projects can direct you to the websites where I found the patterns that are freely available online. Some projects were modified from the original patterns, so they may not look exactly like mine, and some of these projects I made custom, so there are no patterns that can be provided.
