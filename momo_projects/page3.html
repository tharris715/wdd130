Displayed here are the more difficult pieces of work. These take longer to make or take a little more skill that the projects on the easy page. The projects that take the most time by far are the cross-stitch pieces. The Pokemon cross-stitch was the most ambitious project I have taken on. This represents about 3 years of working on it after college classes or work. It was finished around the same time my son was born and now hangs in his room.

Techniques

Some of the patterns in these projects may be a bit more difficult, including the phoenix and dragon. These took more effort than many of the other projects, because of the detail involved. These projects will take longer, and so you should plan ahead if you wish to make them.

Stitches and techniques used for these projects includeThe shell stitch which uses a series of double crochets into the same stitch to create a shell-like pattern. The popcorn stitch which involves making a cluster of stitches in the same stitch to create a raised texture. However, these projects also use many of the easier stitches as well, it's just a matter of how you use stitches that make a project look more complicated.
